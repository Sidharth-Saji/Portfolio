About Me

I am an undergraduate student in Electrical Engineering at IIT Palakkad with a strong interest in analog and mixed-signal electronics, embedded systems, and applied signal processing. I enjoy working at the intersection of theory and hands-on implementation, ranging from circuit design and simulation to hardware validation.

My academic and project experience includes PCB design, hardware debugging, system-level prototyping, and algorithm-to-hardware translation. I am particularly interested in learning industry-grade design practices and contributing to real-world engineering problems.

  • Embedded Systems & Microcontrollers
  • Signal Processing & Hardware Acceleration
